What is Capsule?

To reinforce the importance of keeping memories in a diary in a world being taken over by electronics, Capsule intends to create a website where users can store a diary of key moments in their lives.

What is an E-Diary?

Electronic Diaries are a way of keeping a copy of a diary on a computer.
Having a diary system helps to see deadlines, allowing you to prioritise work that needs to be completed sooner. It helps to find and arrange dates when everyone is available.
